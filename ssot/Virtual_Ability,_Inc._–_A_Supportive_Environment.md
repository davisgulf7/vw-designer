# Virtual Ability, Inc. – A Supportive Environment

Virtual Ability® is a non-profit corporation that enables people with a wide range of disabilities to thrive in online virtual worlds. 
  Founded in 2007, we offer peer support and a community enriched with educational, social, and entertainment opportunities. 
  We also work with professionals to conduct evidenced-based virtual world research.
