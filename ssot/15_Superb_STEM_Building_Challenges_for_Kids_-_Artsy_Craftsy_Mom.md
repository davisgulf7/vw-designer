## What is STEM?

STEM is an abbreviation of ' [Science](https://artsycraftsymom.com/kitchen-science-projects-for-kids/), Technology, Engineering, Mathematics. It is an educational approach that combines the teaching of all these subjects through activities. A more recent version is STEAM, which also includes art as an important component of educational development.

You're probably wondering what the big deal is since we've had all these subjects at school for generations now. However, that's different from STEM; STEM doesn't teach these subjects separately, it creates an integrated learning approach combining all these concepts together.

That's not all, STEM isn't about reading textbooks or rote learning; it is all about learning through doing and seeing how these concepts apply in everyday lives.

## What are the Benefits of STEM?

Here are some of the benefits of STEM learning for children:

Encourages children to gather information and make their own inferences

Helps kids put their knowledge into practice through activities

Encourages natural scientific curiosity

Improves logical reasoning and problem-solving skills

Shows kids how scientific concepts connect to events in our daily life

When done in collaboration with other kids, STEM inculcates skills like teamwork & communication

Encourages more children to pursue higher studies or careers in STEM fields

## Is there a National STEM Day?

National STEM Day is celebrated every year on **Nov.** **8** to encourage kids to explore their interests in the fields of science, technology, engineering, art, and math.
