## Overview

You can directly connect your [Learning Management System (LMS)](https://support.clever.com/hc/s/articles/000001609) to Clever LMS Connect from your Clever Dashboard! This article will provide you with an overview of the setup process.

**  Please note:** You can connect your LMS only if one or more of your Clever applications is available to connect (otherwise, you'll see the opportunity to join our waitlist).

If you're on the waitlist, you'll get notified once one of your connected apps builds a Clever LMS Connect integration.

## Setting up your Clever LMS Connection

Click the drop-down for the LMS you'd like to connect.

If you use Schoology as your LMS, you will be required to set up LTI SSO. Failing to set up LTI SSO will prevent gradebook and assignment syncs from functioning. If you use Canvas as your LMS, it is highly recommended that you use LTI SSO for the best LMS Connect experience.

Connect with Schoology

From the [Clever Dashboard > Applications > LMS Connect](https://schools.clever.com/lms-connect) page and click **Connect your LMS**. We currently support three LMSs: Canvas, Schoology, and Google Classroom. 

Select your Learning Management System (LMS) & click **Next: Connect your LMS.** At this time, it's only possible to connect one LMS. 

Before you can finish connecting your LMS, Clever must prepare your data. You'll see a loading bar in the bottom corner showing the progress, and you'll also get an email when the process is done. In the meantime, you can select any apps you would like Clever LMS Connect to be available for.

When ready, you can continue the setup process by clicking Continue setup on your [LMS Connect page](https://schools.clever.com/lms-connect?source=main_nav).

Click **Next: Log into Schoology.** 

Configure your LMS source.

Enter your LMS URL or check **No Custom URL.**

Connect your LMS account. 

Sign into your LMS. Each LMS may have a different sign-in method.

Once signed in, you'll see confirmation that your account is connected: 

Scroll down in the window and click **Validate Configuration.**

**  **

You'll now see the validation results and can click **Continue Setup**. 

After your LMS is successfully connected, Clever will begin account matching to connect accounts. This can take a few days. You'll be notified via email when it's complete. You can send connection requests to your available applications while matching is in progress, so there's no need to wait!

** 

Please note:** We match IDs between your LMS and Clever. If those IDs don't match up, we'll let you know and help you create and upload a simple file to map the IDs and get everything working smoothly. 

On your LMS Connect page, you'll see that matching is in progress.

Please note that when using Clever LMS Connect with Schoology, Clever requires you to utilize LTI SSO for the best experience. [Learn more here](https://support.clever.com/hc/s/articles/000001647).

Connect with Google Classroom

To ensure a quality data matching experience with Google Classroom, Clever requires districts to connect with Clever's Google Classroom roster import application. As Google Classroom allows teachers to modify their own classrooms, this presents a challenge with maintaining data parity for Clever LMS Connect if a district isn't using Clever to roster Google Classroom courses. Please start your setup by [adding the Google Classroom roster import application](https://schools.clever.com/applications/add/google-classroom-rostering). For more information, [click here](https://support.clever.com/hc/s/articles/000001503), or reach out to Clever Support.

For Schoology, Canvas, and Google Classroom, gradebook and assignment syncing is available for any app you've established an LMS connection with.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with Clever LMS Connect, follow these steps to resolve them.

**Clever logs you out automatically during setup**

Logging out automatically is expected when a user session expires for security reasons.

**INTEGRATION_CREATION_ERROR** 

This error can occur on Edlink's side during setup. Please wait 30 minutes and then try again. If you continue to see the error, please contact our team so we can review the status.

**Pending_authorization** 

This will appear when your LMS is created in Clever and synced to Edlink but you have not completed the setup to authorize yet. To clear this, you'll need to continue the setup flow from your Clever Dashboard.

**Edlink 400 login error**

This typically means that your account isn't matched. Please wait 30 minutes and reattempt setup.

**Looks like you already have an Edlink account. Enter your Edlink password to connect it with your Clever identity.**

To resolve this issue:

If your school district has an existing Edlink integration:

You are unable to have Edlink connected with two vendors at once. Please reach out to Clever Support to let our team know how you would like us to handle the LMS Connect setup.

If you're locked out of your account, contact Clever Support for help. Our team will work with Edlink to unlock your account.

If your school district does not have an existing Edlink integration:

Navigate to [https://ed.link/forgot](https://ed.link/forgot) to reset your password

Enter your password

Click **Confirm**

**Grade and assignment writeback isn't working for a user**

To resolve this issue:

Ensure that you have set up LTI SSO for your LMS. You can learn how to set this up [here](https://support.clever.com/hc/s/articles/000001647?language=en_US).

Check that your user has performed an LTI session from the LMS

If you've confirmed both of these actions, but the assignment writeback is still not working, please contact our support team.
