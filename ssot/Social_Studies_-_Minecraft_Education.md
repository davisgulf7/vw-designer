# Social Studies

Immerse your learners in history, culture, and civics with Minecraft Education! Explore cultural heritage around the world, bring economics to life, delve into ancient civilizations, and so much more. Each world and build challenge is accompanied by a ready-to-teach lesson plan. Happy trails!

### Peter is Here!

Step into the heart of history! Journey through 2,000 years of architectural innovation, discover how new technologies can restore ancient wonders, and explore the history of St. Peter's Basilica.

###  Get started Peace Builders

Follow the stories of Nobel Peace Prize Laureates as you help them avoid and mitigate conflict, created in partnership with the Nobel Peace Center and Games for Change.

##  Learn more Lesson Collections

Explore groups of lessons that focus on specific subjects, events, or themes within social studies. 


 Exploring Ancient Egypt 

 Enter the world of Ancient Egypt and interact with one of the earliest world powers, learning about its fascinating geography, advanced economy, governing structure, iconic architecture, and unique culture. 

 Exploring World War I 

 Use these five lessons and Minecraft world to help study World War I in your classroom. Lessons cover the causes of war, communication, strategy, and remembrance. 

 Social Emotional Learning 

 These worlds and activities support Social and Emotional Learning. Concepts include emotional well-being, empathy, and making responsible decisions. 

 Civic Engagement and Citizenship 

 Explore how normal people can make a difference through politics and protest. 

 World Cities and Civilizations 

 Travel around the world and across time to discover history firsthand. 

 Indigenous Cultures and History 

 Experience the stories, history, and culture of First Nations people from around the world. 

## Social Studies Build Challenges

These creative challenges provide easy-to-implement entry points to social studies topics.

 Hall of Inventions 

 Learn about key innovations across history and build your own! 

 Mayan Mystery 

 Explore the historic ruins of Tulum and find clues to a secret room that can only be accessed by using coding skills. 

 Historical Homes 

 Travel through time in this build challenge and explore architecture from ancient, medieval, and modern times.
